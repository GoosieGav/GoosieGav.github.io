About

Ichiban Buffet offers an expansive selection of Chinese and Asian-inspired dishes in a modern, family-friendly setting. Enjoy made-fresh classics, a full sushi bar, and rotating chef specials.

  • Large buffet with sushi bar
  • Casual, family-friendly dining
  • Carryout and delivery available
  • Ample parking and accessible entrance
